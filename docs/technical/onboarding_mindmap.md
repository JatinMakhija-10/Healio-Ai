# Onboarding Layer Architecture

## Executive Summary
A dual-pathway onboarding system where patients undergo a 8-step holistic health profiling (Medical + Ayurvedic) for personalized care, while doctors complete a 4-step professional verification and availability setup for practice approval.

## Topic-Subtopic Tree Mindmap

- **Onboarding Layer**
  - **Patient Flow** (Holistic Health Profiling)
    - **Step 1: Identity & Basics**
      - Full Name
      - Age & Gender
    - **Step 2: Care Preferences**
      - Modern Medicine
      - Ayurveda
      - Yoga & Exercise
      - Home Remedies
    - **Step 3: Vitals & Lifestyle**
      - Body Metrics (Height, Weight)
      - Habits (Smoking, Alcohol)
      - Activity Level & Diet
    - **Step 4: Medical History**
      - Existing Conditions (Diabetes, etc.)
      - Allergies
    - **Step 5: Background & Risk**
      - Family History
      - Sleep Patterns & Occupation
      - Recent Surgeries
    - **Step 6: Safety Screening**
      - Pregnancy Check
      - Kidney/Liver Health
      - Current Medications & BP
    - **Step 7: Emergency Protocol**
      - Emergency Contact Details
    - **Step 8: Consent & Processing**
      - Legal/Privacy Consent
      - **Automated Analysis**
        - Health Risk Profile (BMI, Cardiovascular Risk)
        - Ayurvedic Prakriti Assessment (Vata/Pitta/Kapha)
  
  - **Doctor Flow** (Credentialing & Setup)
    - **Step 1: Personal Profile**
      - Identity (Name, Phone)
      - Professional Bio
    - **Step 2: Professional Credentials**
      - Specializations (e.g., Cardiology, Ayurveda)
      - Qualifications & Experience
      - Medical License Number
    - **Step 3: Verification Documents**
      - License Certificate Upload
      - Profile Photo
    - **Step 4: Practice Configuration**
      - Consultation Fee Setup
      - Session Duration (15-60 mins)
      - Weekly Availability Scheduler
    - **Step 5: Application Review**
      - Submission to Admin
      - Pending Approval State
